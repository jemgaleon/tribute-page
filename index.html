Elon Reeve Musk

An engineer and technology entrepreneur

Here's a timeline of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1995 - starts Zip2 with brother, coding it himself.
  • 1999 - starts X.com - an online bank (with his own money).
  • 2000 - merges with Confinity forming Paypal.
  • 2002 - starts SpaceX to build his own rockets (with own money).
  • 2004 - invests in Tesla, suggesting JB and Tesla founders work together.
  • 2006 - becomes 1st investor in SolarCity (cousins' company.
  • 2013 - publishes the design of Hyperloop high-speed rail.
  • 2014 - starts building the Gigafactory to mass produce batteries.
  • 2015 - unveils Powerwall.
  • 2016 - launches his largest rocket Falcon Heavy.
  • 2020 - first Hyperloop trains start running.
  • 2025 - Mars Colonial Transporter launches.
